## üì§ Upload & Configure (Steps 2-6)

### Step 2: Upload Your Yard Photo
**Page:** Upload  
**What you'll see:** Upload area highlighted  
**What to do:** Click or drag to upload a photo of your current yard  
**Why it matters:** The AI needs to see your space to create an accurate design

### Step 3: Choose Your Design Style
**Page:** Upload  
**What you'll see:** Style gallery highlighted  
**What to do:** Select one or more style references from the curated gallery  
**Pro tip:** You can select multiple styles to blend aesthetics!

### Step 4: Pick Your Aesthetic
**Page:** Upload  
**What you'll see:** Aesthetic selector buttons highlighted  
**What to do:** Choose from Modern, Traditional, Tropical, Mediterranean, etc.  
**Why it matters:** This sets the overall mood and feel of your design

### Step 5: Add Your Preferences
**Page:** Upload  
**What you'll see:** Text input area highlighted  
**What to do:** Type specific features you want (fire pit, water features, etc.)  
**Examples:**
- "I want a fire pit area with seating"
- "Low maintenance plants only"
- "Include a stone walkway to the backyard"

### Step 6: Generate Your Design
**Page:** Upload  
**What you'll see:** Generate button highlighted  
**What to do:** Click "Generate Design" to start the AI process  
**Time:** Takes about 30-60 seconds

## üéì Key Concepts

### What is RAG-Powered Budgeting?
RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) means our AI searches a real database of products and materials to give you accurate, real-world pricing. It's not just guessing‚Äîit's matching your design to actual items you can buy!

### Why Multiple Renders?
Different angles and lighting help you visualize how your space will look at different times of day and from different viewpoints.

### Can I Edit After Generation?
Yes! You can always create a new design with adjusted preferences. Each generation is unique.

## üí° Pro Tips
